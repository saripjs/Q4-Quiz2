Characters In Noli Me Tangere
CRISOSTOMO IBBARA
  • He is the novio of Maria Clara.
  • He lost his father while he was studying in Europe.
  • He continued to fulfill the plans of his father, Don Rafael.
MARIA CLARA
  • She is the novia of Crisostomo Ibarra.
  • She stayed in the convent to study for 7 years.
  • She is the daughter of Kapitan Tiago and the niece of Tiya Isabel.
PADRE DAMASO
  • He is known as the “ninong” of Maria Clara.
  • He thinks highly of himself.
  • He doesn’t know how to speak Filipino even if he stayed in the country for how many years.
PILOSOPO TASIO
  • He was considered “crazy” by the lower class Filipinos in San Diego.
  • He has a lot of insights and perspectives about every topic.
  • He spent all his time and money on books and forgot about the sayings of his mother.
PADRE SALVI
  • He replaced Padre Damaso and became the new curate of San Diego.
  • He became interested in Maria Clara.
  • He is known as the curate who always gets sick after every mass.
